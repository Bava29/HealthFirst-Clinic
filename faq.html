General FAQs

How can I book an appointment? +
You can book an appointment through our website or by calling our clinic.
What are your working hours? +
We are open Monday to Saturday from 9 AM to 6 PM.
Do you accept walk-in patients? +
Yes, walk-ins are accepted based on doctor availability.
Appointments

Booking & Scheduling

How can I book an appointment? +
You can book an appointment through our website by filling out the form or by calling our clinic directly.
Can I reschedule my appointment? +
Yes, you can reschedule your appointment by contacting us or using the reschedule option available in your booking confirmation.
How do I cancel my appointment? +
You can cancel your appointment through our website or by contacting our support team before your scheduled time.
Will I receive appointment confirmation? +
Yes, once your appointment is booked, you will receive a confirmation via email or SMS with all the details.
Can I book an appointment for someone else? +
Yes, you can book an appointment on behalf of a family member or friend by entering their details during booking.
What should I bring for my appointment?
Please carry your ID, previous medical records, and any prescriptions to ensure a smooth consultation.
Payments

Billing & Insurance

What payment methods do you accept? +
We accept cash, debit/credit cards, UPI payments, and secure online transactions for your convenience.
Do you accept health insurance? +
Yes, we accept selected insurance providers. Please contact our front desk to confirm your eligibility.
Will I receive a payment receipt? +
Yes, you will receive a detailed receipt after every payment via email or printed copy.
Is there a refund policy? +
Refunds are processed based on our clinic policy. Please contact our support team for assistance.
Are there any hidden charges? +
No, we maintain complete transparency. All charges will be clearly explained before treatment.
Treatments

Services & Procedures

What treatments do you offer? +
We provide a wide range of treatments including cardiology, dermatology, general consultations, and preventive care services.
How long does a treatment usually take? +
Treatment duration depends on the condition and procedure. Most consultations take 20–30 minutes, while specialized treatments may take longer.
Are follow-up visits required? +
Yes, follow-up visits may be required depending on your condition to monitor progress and ensure effective treatment.
Do you provide emergency treatment? +
Yes, we offer emergency support for critical cases. Please contact us immediately for urgent medical assistance.
Can I consult a specialist directly? +
Yes, you can book appointments directly with specialists based on availability.
Doctors

Our Medical Experts

Are your doctors certified and experienced?
Yes, all our doctors are highly qualified, certified, and experienced in their respective fields.
Can I choose a specific doctor?
Yes, you can select your preferred doctor while booking an appointment based on availability.
Do your doctors provide online consultation?
Yes, we offer online consultations for selected services to make healthcare more accessible.
How experienced are your specialists?
Our specialists have years of experience and are trained to handle a wide range of medical conditions.
Can I consult multiple doctors for the same issue?
Yes, you can consult multiple specialists for a second opinion if needed.
Still Have Questions?

Our team is here to help you. Contact us anytime or book an appointment with our doctors.
